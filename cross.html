The Cross

The cross is the first step of the CFOP method that solves the cross on the bottom.

Beginner cross (cross in beginner CFOP) means building the cross in (ideally) no more than 12 moves. Building the cross is mostly intuitive, so I recommend watching this video to learn how to do it quickly.
