4x4 Cube

The 4x4 is a fun challenge to take on once you've mastered the 3x3. As you may have guessed, it's much harder.

Here is a 4x4 video tutorial I made. The idea is this: you solve the center colors and edges so that it's reduced to a 3x3, then solve it like a 3x3.
